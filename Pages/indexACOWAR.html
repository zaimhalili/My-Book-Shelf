A Court of Wings and Ruin

699 Pages

Summary: Feyre returns to the Spring Court as a spy, determined to gather information on Tamlin and the King of Hybern’s plans. Using her wits and new powers, she plays a dangerous game to undermine their alliance from within. As war looms over Prythian, Feyre and Rhysand rally the High Lords of the other courts, struggling to forge fragile alliances against Hybern’s forces. Battles rage across the land, and Feyre must harness every bit of her strength and cunning to protect those she loves and stop Hybern’s brutal invasion. With fierce loyalty, sacrifice, and shocking betrayals, Feyre and her friends fight for the future of both the fae and mortal realms, leading to a climactic confrontation that will change Prythian forever.

Main Characters:

  • Feyre - High Lady of the Night Court; cunning, powerful, and willing to risk everything for her loved ones.
  • Rhysand - High Lord of the Night Court; unwavering in his support of Feyre and determined to protect Prythian.
  • Azriel - Stealthy spymaster; silent, deadly, and loyal beyond question.
  • Cassian - Commander of the Night Court’s armies; fearless warrior and steadfast friend.
  • Mor - Morally strong and unyielding; essential in rallying allies and fighting Hybern’s forces.
  • Amren - Ancient and enigmatic; her true nature becomes key in the final battle.
  • Lucien - Tamlin’s emissary; torn between loyalties, but ultimately vital to Feyre’s cause.
  • King of Hybern - Ruthless mastermind of the invasion; determined to shatter the wall and enslave humans.
  • Tamlin - High Lord of the Spring Court; his actions and choices have lasting consequences for the war’s outcome.
