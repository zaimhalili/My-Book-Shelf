The Name of the Wind

662 Pages

Summary: In a world of myth and magic, Kvothe, a gifted young man, grows up in a traveling troupe before tragedy strikes and leaves him an orphan. Struggling to survive on the streets, Kvothe’s intelligence and determination drive him to seek admission to the prestigious University, where he hopes to uncover the truth about the mysterious Chandrian who killed his family. As Kvothe navigates the dangers of the University, he learns the art of Naming, earns a place among the scholars, and starts to build his legend. His journey takes him through love, music, rivalries, and ancient secrets—each step bringing him closer to the power and knowledge he seeks, but also deeper into a destiny fraught with darkness.

Main Characters:

  • Kvothe - Prodigy and storyteller; brilliant, ambitious, and haunted by his past.
  • Denna - Elusive and enchanting; a gifted singer with secrets of her own.
  • Bast - Kvothe’s mysterious apprentice; charming, mischievous, and more than he appears.
  • Ambrose - Wealthy and arrogant student; Kvothe’s bitter rival at the University.
  • Elxa Dal - Master Namer at the University; wise and respected teacher.
  • Devi - Brilliant but dangerous moneylender; known as a “demon” for her fierce reputation.
  • Master Lorren - Stern and meticulous Keeper of the Archives; guards the University’s vast knowledge.
  • Chandrian - Legendary beings of dark power; enigmatic, feared, and central to Kvothe’s quest for answers.
