The Way of Kings

1007 Pages

Summary: On the storm-battered world of Roshar, war rages on the Shattered Plains as the Alethi armies fight a protracted and brutal conflict against the Parshendi. Amidst the chaos, three central figures begin journeys that will shape the fate of their world. Kaladin, once a gifted surgeon’s apprentice and now a slave, struggles to survive in the bridge crews—where men are little more than fodder. As he discovers newfound powers, he grapples with despair and seeks purpose beyond the battlefield. Highprince Dalinar Kholin, haunted by visions during highstorms, questions the true purpose of the war and the ancient codes of honor his house once lived by. His search for meaning could either unite or doom the fractured Alethi leadership. Meanwhile, Shallan Davar, a young noblewoman desperate to save her family, apprentices under Jasnah Kholin to steal a powerful magical device, only to uncover secrets about the lost Knights Radiant and the coming Desolation. As their paths converge, each must confront dark truths about themselves and their world—setting events in motion that could awaken ancient forces long forgotten.

Main Characters:

  • Kaladin - Former soldier and slave; determined, resilient, and discovering mysterious new powers.
  • Dalinar Kholin - Honorable highprince; strong leader torn between duty, visions, and unraveling political intrigue.
  • Shallan Davar - Clever and determined scholar; talented artist hiding dangerous secrets.
  • Szeth-son-son-Vallano - Assassin bound by an Oathstone; haunted by guilt and forced to kill against his will.
  • Jasnah Kholin - Brilliant and unyielding scholar; a heretic who seeks the truth about Roshar’s ancient past.
  • Sadeas - Ambitious and ruthless highprince; driven by his own thirst for power and glory.
  • Rock - Horneater member of Bridge Four; good-natured, strong, and a loyal friend to Kaladin.
  • Sylphrena (Syl) - A spren bonded to Kaladin; playful, curious, and key to his growing abilities.
