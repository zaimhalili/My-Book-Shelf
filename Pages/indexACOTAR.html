A Court of Thorns and Roses

432 Pages

Summary: When Feyre, a mortal huntress, kills a wolf in the woods, a beast-like fae named Tamlin takes her to his magical land, Prythian, as punishment. At his Spring Court estate, Feyre discovers a world of beauty, magic, and danger—and slowly falls in love with Tamlin. But Tamlin and his court are cursed by a powerful fae tyrant, Amarantha, who rules from beneath the mountain. Feyre learns she may be the key to breaking the curse. To save Tamlin, she endures deadly trials, solves riddles, and suffers heartbreak under Amarantha's rule. In the end, Feyre breaks the curse and is resurrected as a High Fae, changed forever—physically and emotionally.

Main Characters:

  • Feyre - Mortal huntress turned High Fae; brave, loyal, and determined to protect those she loves.
  • Tamlin - High Lord of the Spring Court; strong, protective, and cursed by dark magic.
  • Lucien - Tamlin's witty, scarred friend; loyal and sharp-tongued.
  • Rhysand - High Lord of the Night Court; mysterious, powerful, and cunning.
  • Amarantha - Sadistic ruler under the mountain; cruel and manipulative.
